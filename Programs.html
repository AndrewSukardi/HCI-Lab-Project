ORBIT EARTH

OVERVIEW

This program will take up to 7 person, to see orbit of the earth and see earth in difference view. Mission will provide the best view as possible to get the best experience. Rocket that will be used is dragon and will be fully orbit with custom path until flight duration is over.

Flight duration will be 60 - 90 minute

Training duration will be approximately 2 month before flight

EARTH TO EARTH

OVERVIEW

This program will change how commercial flight work. The program will use rocket instead of plane to go one place to another place. This will speed up travel time up to 80% than commercial plane. With rocket that flight in space outside earth will make less the lack of friction as well as turbulence and weather.

Flight duration will be approximately takes up to 30 minute for the longest trip.
